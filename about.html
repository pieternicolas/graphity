ABOUT US

Graphity Design is a freelance graphic design studio passionate about visual communication through great design established in 2015 by Nicole Jurmann, based in Singapore. Specialized in corporate identity, branding, literature, packaging, web design, photography, and advertising. We are commited to produce innovative and original design that communicates effectively. Client is number one, we generate our best as possible within budget and time.

Starting from small but perfectly formed allows us to compete, while our depth of experience and skill ensures our ability to handle the pace and demand of large projects. We help our clients to increase sales, excite customers and communicate more effectively.
